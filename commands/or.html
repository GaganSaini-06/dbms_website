OR OPERATOR

Definition: The OR operator in SQL is used to filter records where **at least one condition** is true.

Syntax:

SELECT * FROM table_name WHERE condition1 OR condition2;

Example:

SELECT * FROM students WHERE Name = 'Virat' OR Course = 'BBA';
